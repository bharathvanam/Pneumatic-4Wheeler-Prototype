# Pneumatic 4-Wheeler Prototype

## Overview
This project demonstrates the design and fabrication of an air-powered pneumatic 4-wheeler vehicle.  
It integrates mechanical, pneumatic, and electrical systems to showcase sustainable mobility solutions.

## Objectives
- Develop a working prototype using compressed air as the primary energy source.
- Integrate mechanical design with pneumatic actuation.
- Explore energy-efficient alternatives to conventional fuel-based vehicles.

## Tools & Technologies
- **CAD Modeling**: AutoCAD, Fusion 360
- **Simulation**: Ansys Workbench
- **Fabrication**: Welding, machining, prototyping
- **Programming**: Basic C/Python for automation and control

## Key Features
- Pneumatic drive system for eco-friendly operation.
- Lightweight chassis designed for stability and durability.
- Integration of electrical components for control and monitoring.

## Results
- Successfully fabricated and tested the prototype.
- Demonstrated feasibility of pneumatic energy in small-scale vehicles.
- Identified areas for improvement in efficiency and scalability.

## Future Scope
- Optimize pneumatic efficiency with advanced valve systems.
- Explore hybrid integration with renewable energy sources.
- Scale design for industrial applications.
